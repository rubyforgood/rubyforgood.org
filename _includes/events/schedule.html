2024 Event Schedule

We'll be using the Ruby for Good Slack extensively during the event. Sign up now so you don't miss any announcements, have an early opportunity to meet all your new friends, and get all the Ruby insider info!

Below is a tentative schedule for our 2024 Ruby for Good event. Any last-minute changes will be communicated to attendees via Slack and updated here shortly thereafter.

Thursday, May 30th

  • 4:00 pm - Visit with team leads, familiarize yourself with each project’s nonprofit and their mission, ask about particular projects, and get a sense of technical requirements. Consider this an opportunity to go into a little more depth with projects before you choose to commit to one in particular. This portion is optional, but you may find it helpful in making your decision!
  • 5:00 pm - Event kickoff and announcements
  • 5:30 pm - Team pitches, team selection, initial team discussion
  • 6:00 pm - Dinner with your team (and others!)
  • 7:00 pm – Teams set up environments, clone down repos, etc.
  • 8:30 pm or earlier if teams have everything ready to go for Friday - Board games, karaoke, socializing, and other random fun

Friday, May 31st

  • 8:00 am - Breakfast
  • 9:00 am - Work on projects
  • 12:00 pm - Lunch
  • 1:00 pm - Work on projects
  • 5:00 pm - Dinner
  • 6:30 pm – PHOTOS (entire group, individual teams, Flatiron students/alumni, and fridayhug)
  • 7:00 pm - Werewolf, board games, karaoke, campfire, s'mores, socializing, fun!

Saturday, June 1st

  • 8:00 am - Breakfast
  • 9:00 am - Work on projects
  • 12:00 pm - Lunch
  • 1:00 pm - Work on projects
  • 5:00 pm - Dinner
  • 6:30 pm - Career chat -- If you are looking, you or your company is hiring, or you just want to ask or help answer career questions this is the place for you
  • 7:30 pm - Werewolf, board games, karaoke, campfire, s'mores, socializing, fun!

Sunday, June 2nd

  • 8:00 am - Breakfast
  • 9:00 am - Work on projects
  • 11:00 am - Demos (if your project isn’t finished, demo what you can!)
