Railsconf 2022

Tech conferences are fun! We feel that they are even more fun when you have a lot of friends attending the conference with you -- especially if those friends are great people! We might be biased (okay, we're totally biased) but we think that Gooders (that's what Ruby for Good folks call ourselves) are the absolutely best people around. We want to invite everyone to join us the day before Railsconf to do some good together and then for some socializing! This way you'll have a great network for friends to talk about which sessions to attend, discover the best nearby coffee shops; this is an important one since Portland has a lot of amazing coffee shops, and you'll have the best people to eat lunch with.

What will we be doing?

How The Volunteering Will Work

We're going to head to the Portland Diaper Bank and provide them with some much needed assistance helping them assemble some diapers and period supplies in to bundles that are easy to distribute. It is a lot of fun and in addition to doing good we'll be doing some great socializing.

How Will We Get There

We will meet up at the conference hotel, the Double Tree Portland, at a time TBD. We currently don't have the exact time and we'll update this as soon as we know but it is looking like it will be leaving from the hotel at noon. We will coordinate rideshares and head to the volunteering venue.

How Long Will It Last

We will spend approximately one to two hours volunteering at the Portland Diaper Bank after which we will head to a local restaurant/brewery for lunch and socializing. We have some super fun ice breaker conversation topics lined up and we're really excited for everyone to make their new #rubyfriends.

Let Us Know You Are Coming

We would love to get an accurate headcount to let the Diaper Bank knows how many folks to expect so join us in our slack and say hello in the #railsconf channel and let us know you are coming. If you are unable to join us in slack send us an email to info@rubyforgood.org.

Want to support us?

If helping a group of rubyists doing some good is something your company would be interested in supporting get in touch with us at info@rubyforgood.org. We'd love a sponsor for transportation and lunch for the volunteers after.
